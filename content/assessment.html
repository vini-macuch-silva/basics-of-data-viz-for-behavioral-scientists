Course assessment

Students will be assessed on the basis of the following components:

  • [10%] Presence and class participation;

  • [40%] Completion of a homework assignment between week 1 and week 2. The homework will consist of exploring the rating data and producing a visual report of the most interesting/ relevant data patterns;

  • [50%] Completion of a final assignment consisting of drafting a “results” section of a scientific paper which includes publication-ready visualizations of both the rating data and the reaction time data.
